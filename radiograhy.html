Radiography Services at Muhondo Health Center

Radiography is an essential medical service offered at Muhondo Health Center. It involves the use of medical imaging techniques to diagnose and treat various medical conditions.

At Muhondo Health Center, our radiography services include:

  • X-rays: X-ray imaging is commonly used to visualize bones and diagnose fractures, joint dislocations, and other skeletal abnormalities.
  • Ultrasound: Ultrasound imaging uses high-frequency sound waves to produce images of internal organs, tissues, and blood flow. It is often used in obstetrics to monitor fetal development and during diagnostic procedures.
  • CT Scan (Computed Tomography): CT scans provide detailed cross-sectional images of the body, helping diagnose conditions such as tumors, infections, and internal injuries.
  • Mammography: Mammography is a specialized type of X-ray used for breast cancer screening and diagnosis.
  • Fluoroscopy: Fluoroscopy is a real-time imaging technique used to visualize moving internal structures such as the digestive tract, blood vessels, and joints.

Our experienced radiology team utilizes state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to ensure accurate diagnosis and treatment planning for our patients.
