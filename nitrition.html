Better Nutrition: Common Foods for Health

Fruits and Vegetables

Fruits and vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that promote good health. Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables in your diet to ensure you get a wide range of nutrients.

Examples include:

  • Spinach: Rich in iron and vitamins A and C.
  • Blueberries: Packed with antioxidants and vitamin C.
  • Broccoli: High in fiber, vitamins K and C, and folate.
  • Oranges: A great source of vitamin C and potassium.
  • Carrots: Loaded with beta-carotene, fiber, and vitamins A and K.

Whole Grains

Whole grains provide fiber, vitamins, and minerals that are essential for good health. Choose whole grain options over refined grains for better nutrition.

Examples include:

  • Oats: Rich in soluble fiber, which can help lower cholesterol levels.
  • Quinoa: A complete protein source and high in fiber.
  • Brown rice: A good source of fiber and various nutrients.
  • Whole wheat bread: Provides fiber, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Barley: Rich in fiber and antioxidants.
