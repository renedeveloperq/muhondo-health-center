Maternity Services and Care at Muhondo Health Center

Prenatal Care

Muhondo Health Center offers comprehensive prenatal care to ensure the health and well-being of expectant mothers and their babies. Our prenatal care services include:

  • Regular check-ups and monitoring throughout pregnancy
  • Ultrasound scans to assess fetal development
  • Nutritional counseling and guidance
  • Screening for gestational diabetes, hypertension, and other pregnancy-related conditions

Delivery and Postpartum Care

Our experienced healthcare team provides expert care and support during labor, delivery, and the postpartum period. Our delivery and postpartum care services include:

  • Safe and comfortable delivery facilities
  • Skilled obstetricians and midwives to assist with childbirth
  • Pain management options, including epidurals and natural childbirth techniques
  • Immediate newborn care, including assessment, warmth, and breastfeeding support
  • Postpartum check-ups for mothers and babies to monitor recovery and ensure optimal health

We offer antenatal classes and educational programs to prepare expectant mothers and their partners for childbirth and parenthood. These classes cover topics such as:

  • Healthy pregnancy practices
  • Childbirth preparation and relaxation techniques
  • Breastfeeding and newborn care
  • Postpartum recovery and self-care

At Muhondo Health Center, we are committed to providing high-quality maternity services and compassionate care to support mothers and families throughout the journey of pregnancy, childbirth, and beyond.
